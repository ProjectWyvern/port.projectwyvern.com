The ERC20 token port snapshot will be taken at block 310000.

The UTXO set at that block will be used for the ERC20 token port, meaning that you'll be able to turn your Wyvern into WYV ERC20 tokens at redeem.projectwyvern.com.

In order to receive ERC20 tokens, you need to hold your Wyvern in a wallet you control. If you hold Wyvern on an exchange, you need to withdraw it prior to block 310000.
